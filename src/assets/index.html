Course Levels

LF Commence

Commence your language learning journey with a highly curated curriculum in accordance to DELE level A1.

  • Introduce yourself and others
  • Interact in a simple conversation
  • Introduction to Spanish Culture
  • Introduction to Language Career Avenues
LF Engage

Dive deeper into the world of languages with LF Engage. Start speaking with a course curriculum that covers both A1 & A2.

  • Can understand familiar topics
  • Can communicate routine tasks
  • Can describe your background Understanding Spanish Culture
  • Understanding Language Career Avenues
LF Immerse ( Recommended )

Become Competent In Spanish and immerse yourself into Spanish culture. Learn tons of vocabulary and familiarise yourself with new interesting grammatical concepts. Covering everything right from A1 to B1, our mentors will help you understand how you can start making money with the language skillset you now possess. Also, get a paid internship at the end of the course.

  • Can understand familiar topics
  • Can communicate routine tasks
  • Can describe your background Understanding Spanish Culture
  • Understanding Language Career Avenues
LF Leap

Take a leap with us and be fluent in Spanish. The minimum criteria for a lot of companies to hire Language professionals are B2 Certified Individuals. Starting from A1 to B2, youll not only learn how to speak like a native but also secure a high-paying job. Our mentors will also educate you on how you can be a freelancer and work across different language domains.

  • Can talk with native speakers
  • Can understand complex text
  • Can write and explain viewpoints
  • Can express yourself fluently
  • Can use the language creatively
  • Industry-based assignments
  • Immersive Cultural experience from values, sports to Festivals
